- The memo must read as neutral partner-facing analysis, not advocacy.
- The strongest near-term injunctive leverage may come from the USB-copy events even if the covenant is only partly enforceable.
- The legal structure should separate statutory validity questions from reasonableness and PI strategy.

## What must be true in v1
